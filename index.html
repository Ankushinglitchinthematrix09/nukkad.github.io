About Nukkad

Our Story

Nukkad is more than just a cafe - it's a celebration of community, culture, and culinary excellence. Located in the vibrant heart of South Delhi, we've created a space where traditional Indian hospitality meets contemporary dining.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a place where people from all walks of life could come together, share stories, and enjoy exceptional food in a warm, welcoming environment.

Every corner of Nukkad tells a story, from our rustic decor that pays homage to traditional Indian street corners to our carefully curated menu that celebrates both classic and innovative flavors.

Community First

We believe in building connections and fostering a sense of belonging for all our guests.

Quality Excellence

Every dish is crafted with precision, using only the finest ingredients and traditional techniques.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to environmental responsibility through sustainable sourcing and eco-friendly practices.

Cultural Heritage

Experience authentic Indian culture through our food, music, and traditional hospitality.
